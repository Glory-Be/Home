Glory Be

Four miles East of Ocho Rios, next to famous Reggae Beach, the enchanting property of Glory Be offers you three sea front cottages with a unique charm in a private, eco friendly wooded setting: a paradise for rest-seekers, nature-lovers, bird watchers, creative spirits,… alike. Glory Be is all about privacy: the property is large enough and has many cozy nooks and crannies so that you rarely run into other people. There is a swimming pool on the property for guests to use as well as a private man-made beach area that makes an idyllic spot for swimming and snorkeling. In walking distance is an art gallery, several restaurants, a jerk center and a grocery. A paradise for artists, snorkelers and swimmers, creative spirits, nature-lovers, rest-seekers and photographers, Glory Be is ideal for bird watchers as well. Many types of humming birds and migratory birds make Glory Be their safe haven. The cottages are situated so there is a true feeling of being alone in paradise. Many of our visitors enjoy swimming and snorkeling or sunbathing from our beautiful beach side patio. Minibuses and taxis make it a short ride to downtown Ocho Rios.

Enchanting Tropical Setting

The property of Glory Be was purchased in 1951 by renowned American born marine artist, Marion Simmons. After the land was bushed out she stood on the edge of the cliff and saw the fabulous view, exclaiming in absolute awe, “Glory Be!” She asked her close friend the English playwright Sir Noel Coward if he thought it was a good name for her new home, he agreed, and so the name stuck. The main house and studio cottage, designed by Simmons, were built by Italians using old bricks and weathered Jamaican hardwood, giving it its unique charm.

Artist Studio

This spacious seaside cottage (1200 sq. ft.), formerly Marion Simmons’ art studio is romantic for two, comfortable for four. In the great room there is a king size antique four poster bed, a sofa, arm chairs, a stocked book case, antiques, original art work and cable T.V. which has radio channels as well. A fully equipped kitchen is in one corner with close access to the veranda. On the veranda are a dining table, chairs and a glider sofa, where you can see spectacular views of the Caribbean Sea. Adjoining the great room is a separate bedroom with two twin beds, a desk and antique safe. The bathroom with a tropical motif has a solar heated shower, toilet and basin and a view of the pool. Ceiling fans and tropical breezes cool the rooms. Next to the veranda are two patios with a charcoal grill and a hammock. The pool is to the right of the front door. Winding stone steps lead to the beach area.

Sunspots Cottage

This gingerbread cottage decorated with original fret work (1000 sq. ft.) is a real Jamaican country cottage. It is ideal for one person, a couple or a small family. There is a bedroom with a queen-size bed, a living room with a futon sofa, a fully equipped kitchen and a bathroom with bathtub. There are two verandas. One has comfortable arm chairs and the outer veranda has a large dining table, where you can relax and enjoy the sea view.

Calypso Cottage

This lofty cottage (1000 sq. ft.) with an amazing sea view and large outside deck is ideal for a couple looking for a romantic retreat. It is attractively furnished and lovingly handcrafted and built. The entrance door, depicting an orange tree, is an original work of art by local artist Jonna Brasch. The décor is tropical with carved pineapples, colorful carved fish, bamboo tiles, bamboo glass panels and cedar shelves and counters. The cottage has an open kitchen with all amenities, a cozy living space and adjacent bedroom with King-sized bed and a bathroom with walk-in shower and skylight. A steep stairway at the foot of the cottage leads to the sea.

Frequently Asked Questions

 How to get from the airport to Glory Be?

You can come on a TourWise bus. Give them your flight information to make a reservation. They’ll take you right to the door of Glory Be. They charge about US$25 per person. (tourwisesales@gmail.com)

For larger groups, we could arrange a driver with a mini bus who could meet you at the airport with a Glory Be Sign. It would be approx. US$120 from Montego Bay to Glory Be. It takes about 2 hours.

 Do you have a cook available?

The cottages are self catering. There are two professional chefs who live near by, you could hire them at your expense.

There are several restaurants in walking distance. Chinese food can be delivered. 

 What about cleaning/ staff?

There will be a helper to clean and make beds 3 days a week. We have a gardener, who keeps the pool and beach area clean. Both of them will check and remove your garbage. It would be nice if you could leave them a weekly tip. 

 Are the cottages close together?

The property is quite private. There are three cottages on the property. We live in the main house which is between the Simmons Artist Studio Cottage and the Sunspots and Calypso cottages. All cottages are positioned in such a way that they guarantee privacy and a secluded atmosphere. The property is large and has many cozy nooks and crannies so that you rarely run into the other people on the property.

 Do you have good beaches closeby?

Glory Be has a beach area with a ladder and nice swimming spot. There is good snorkeling right in front of Glory Be. We provide beach towels, snorkel gear, life jackets,… If the sea is calm, you can swim (or also walk) to Reggae Beach next door. James Bond beach is not far away, and Turtle Beach is right in Ocho Rios.

 Where can I shop?

There is a small grocery store with basics in walking distance from Glory Be. We are happy to take guests to a larger supermarket in Ocho Rios (4 miles away) for initial shopping and/or when we go there for our shopping.

We arrive very late. Could we arrange to have some provisions in the house when we arrive?

The cottages are stocked with basic initial breakfast items. More groceries can be purchased at your expense. 

 Which airport is best to arrive at?

It is best to fly into Montego Bay. Montego Bay is not that much closer, but it is a straighter and easier drive than from Kingston. Montego Bay is also more geared to tourist transportation.

 Is there a washing machine on site? 

We have a washing machine in the main house. We have a maid who comes 3 days a week and laundry can be done on those days. Sheets and towels are washed once a week. Any extra personal laundry would be an extra US$10 per load.

 Can you recommend tours/ tour guides? 

In our visitors brochure, we list tour guides and ideas for day trips.
